About Streamsource

Streamsource exists to promote dignity, purpose, hope and new opportunities amongst communities, by equipping them to create, and by inviting them to be a part of something bigger than themselves. What does that look like?

Leveraging Start-ups

We're not talking about your little brother's start-up. We believe in leveraging tech start-ups and the ecosystems they're concieved in for powerful change.

Community Tech Consulting

We help local entrepreneurs create high-tech ecosystems in their communities wherever they are.

Coding Bootcamps

The backbone of any high-tech community is programmers. We've built a world-class coding bootcamp in one of the globe's top start-up cities and it's curriculum is part of our offering to entrepreneurs.

Where we do it

All over the world, but our focus is untapped communities full of potential. All we need is an internet connection and a willing entrepreneur!

For most, the end goal of education is employment. However, most education programs are created in isolation from business. We're bringing these two interdepdent spheres together.
