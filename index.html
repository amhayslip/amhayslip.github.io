About Streamsource

Streamsource exists to promote dignity, purpose, hope and new opportunities to communities, by equipping them to create, and by inviting them to be a part of something bigger than themselves. What does that look like?

Community Tech Consulting

Creating Progammers

Where we do it

All over the world, but our focus is untapped communities full of potential. All we need is an internet connection and a willing entreprenuer!
